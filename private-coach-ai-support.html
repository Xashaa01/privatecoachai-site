FAQ

Can I use the app without an account?

Yes. Tap Continue as Guest on the welcome screen. You can calculate BMI, view plans, and log runs locally. Sign‑in is only required for cloud sync and multi‑device backup.

How do I back up or sync my data?

Sign in from the banner on the main screen or the More tab to enable cloud sync.

How do I delete my account and data?

Inside the app, go to More → Account → Delete Account. Or email xashaa02@gmail.com with the subject “Delete My Account”.

Permissions

We request Health, Location, and Notification permissions only when needed for features you enable (e.g., run tracking or HealthKit sync).
