Privacy Policy

Private Coach AI

Last updated: October 2025

Overview

Private Coach AI (“we”, “our”, “us”) respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. You can use the app in Guest Mode without creating an account.

Information We Collect

  • Health Data (optional): Weight, BMI, steps, workouts via Apple HealthKit — only with your explicit consent.
  • Location Data (optional): GPS during runs for route and distance tracking — only when you enable run tracking.
  • Account Data (optional): Email and password if you create an account for cloud sync.
  • Usage & Diagnostics: App events and crash logs to improve performance. Some data may be processed by Apple or Firebase.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and personalize training insights and weight‑loss analytics.
  • Enable cloud sync and multi‑device backup when signed in.
  • Maintain security, debug issues, and improve features.

Data Storage & Security

  • Health and location data remain on your device unless you sync to the cloud.
  • Account data and cloud‑synced content are stored securely (e.g., Firebase) with encryption in transit and at rest.
  • We do not sell personal data.

Your Choices

  • Use Guest Mode without an account.
  • Grant or revoke HealthKit and Location permissions at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Access, export, or delete your data by contacting xashaa02@gmail.com.
  • Delete your account from More → Account → Delete Account in the app.

Data Retention

We keep cloud data for as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, associated personal data is deleted or anonymized within a reasonable period, subject to legal requirements and backups.

Children’s Privacy

Private Coach AI is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Third‑Party Services

We may use Apple frameworks (e.g., HealthKit) and Firebase for authentication, storage, analytics, and crash reporting. These providers process data as described in their policies.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will update the “Last updated” date and, when appropriate, notify you in‑app.
